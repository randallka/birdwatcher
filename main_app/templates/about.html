About BirdWatcher

BirdWatcher is an App created using Django. I made it for practice but take alot of pride in my work. If you have any suggestions, let me know!


BirdWatcher is a social and informational app for bird-lovers and bird-watchers! Enthusiasts can share locations, sightings and information on different species. Additions I have in mind are:

  • Images
  • external info sources
